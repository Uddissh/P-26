# IngredientsUsedInPotatoCurry
the ingredients you need to cook potato curry are as follows :
1.  5 boilled potatos 🥔🥔🥔🥔🥔
2.  a hollow utencil 🍳
3.  3 ml oil (oil which type of oil, your choice.)
4.  a pan (to take out the curry and it should be hollow)
5.  salt
6.  black pepper
7.  chili
8.  onion
9.  garlic 
10. 300 ml water
